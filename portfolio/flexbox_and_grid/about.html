About us

Sonor Music first opened its doors in 1980 selling and servicing electric and acoustic guitars. After 39 years, we have evolved into a modern music centre serving everyone from amateur to professional musicians. We take pride in our excellent service and totally stand by our products. Our acclaimed music lessons are particularly well known in the industry.



We offer guitar and piano lessons instructed by experienced professional musicians.

Please check out one of our jazz guitar lessons.
